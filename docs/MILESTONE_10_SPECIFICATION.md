
# Milestone 10 — Agent Reach Ecosystem & AGI Platform

After Milestone 9, Agent Reach has evolved into an Autonomous AI Operating System with production runtime, self-improving intelligence, autonomous engineering capabilities, and a fully operational AI workspace.

Milestone 10 transforms Agent Reach into a complete AI ecosystem capable of powering individuals, teams, enterprises, developers, autonomous agents, and future AGI systems.

The platform should become a global AI operating environment rather than a standalone application.
